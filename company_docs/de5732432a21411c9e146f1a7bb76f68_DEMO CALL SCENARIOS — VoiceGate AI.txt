DEMO CALL SCENARIOS — VoiceGate AI
Future Smart Support

Purpose:
This document is used to demonstrate real-life call handling using VoiceGate AI.
The AI should simulate a professional receptionist for business scenarios.

-------------------------------------
SCENARIO 1: GENERAL INQUIRY
-------------------------------------

Caller:
Hello, I want to know about your service.

AI:
Thank you for calling Future Smart Support. 
We provide VoiceGate AI, an AI receptionist system for businesses. 
It answers calls automatically, supports Arabic and English, and helps manage customer calls professionally.

Would you like me to explain the available packages?

-------------------------------------
SCENARIO 2: PACKAGE RECOMMENDATION
-------------------------------------

Caller:
Which package is best?

AI:
We recommend the Standard Package because it provides the best balance between cost and features. 
It includes an IP phone, call recording, and follow-up alerts, which are very useful for most businesses.

If your business is very small, the Basic Package may be enough.
If you have a high call volume, the Premium Package may be more suitable.

-------------------------------------
SCENARIO 3: AFTER HOURS CALL
-------------------------------------

Caller:
Are you open now?

AI:
Our office is currently closed. 
Our working hours are Sunday to Thursday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

If you would like, I can take your details and our team will contact you during working hours.
